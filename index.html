PROFILE

大阪出身、沖縄在住、ダイビング歴10年以上大学時代にダイビングと出会い、 先輩方の影響で水中世界を撮り始める。何気ない海の日常を切り撮ることに魅了され、 大学卒業後は、「海のある暮らし」を求めて沖縄に移住。現在は沖縄の海を拠点に撮影を 続けています。

Born in Osaka, now based in Okinawa. I discovered scuba diving during my university years and was inspired by senior divers to start photographing the underwater world. Captivated by the beauty of everyday scenes in the ocean, I moved to Okinawa after graduation to pursue a life connected to the sea. I now continue my underwater photography with the Okinawan sea as my base.
